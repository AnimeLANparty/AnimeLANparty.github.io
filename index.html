Slice of Life

Do you enjoy life? Then these shows are for you. "Slice of Life" refers to a genre that depicts a character's daily life. Many people consider these shows boring and slow-paced. While it is easy for a slice of life show to be slow-paced, if done right, they can be relaxing and evoke feelings of nostalgia. Take the show Non-Non Biyori. Many of the characters in that show aren't relatable to me. My life is nothing like there's. Yet, I feel nostalgic from watching their childhood, a childhood that I now realize supersecedes many cultural bounds.

Isekai

Do you hate life? Then these shows are for you. "Isekai" refers to a genre that depicts a character being sent to a fantasy, usually away from their usual boring world. What a strange concept this is, and yet, it somehow kinda works. Take Re-Zero for instance, a classic isekai anime. To the usual viewer, seeing this concept would be wild, but yet, it really works. One of the main reasons why is that the juxtaposition between a regular guy and some fantasy crazy world. There's probably some deep metaphorical analogy there, but I'm not doing to get into it.
