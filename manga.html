  • Theodore Gray's The Elements: A Visual Exploration of Every Known Atom in the Universe

    Probably one of the best plots to a manga that I have ever seen. The character variety is so vast it's hard to keep count. The lore being it is so deep and intricate that many people today are still discussing it. And unlike many other mangas out there, this one comes in rich colors that are very eye-catching. Honestly, I don't even know why I'm bringing this masterpiece of narrative storytelling up like you haven't heard of it before. Yeah, you probaby heard references from this manga before in all sorts of different media, but probably haven't even recognized it. It was reference a lot in the hit show Rick and Morty, you probably heard jokes about it in your uni class, and you probably seem someone wearing merch from this franchise. In short, a resounding classic, I applaud the author.
